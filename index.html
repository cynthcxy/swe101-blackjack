The aim of the game is to get as close to 21 points without going over.

You can choose to hit if you want to draw more cards, or stay once you think you have enough points to win against the dealer.

The dealer will then draw cards for their hand. If they have 17 or more, they will not draw.

If you get two cards of the same rank, you can choose to split your cards into 2 hands and play them separately.

However, once you split your cards, you will only get dealt an additional card for each hand then forced to stay while the dealer continues to draw.

Good luck!
